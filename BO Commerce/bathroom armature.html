GMS Water Fixtures

BO Commerce is the official distributor of GMS Water Fixtures.

GMS Water Fixtures, which has an annual production capacity of 1.2 million armatures and 400 thousand valves, continues its activities in Hadımköy, Istanbul, with a team of 135 people. As of now, GMS products have been used in North African countries, Saudi Arabia, Dubai and also Türkiye Republics.

GMS has TS EN 200-TS EN 817 TSE certificates given in accordance with the new norms of Turkish Standards in its own laboratory. It has also documented the principle of quality in production by obtaining the TÜV-CERT certificate. With its SUPERIOR product quality and SUPERIOR service understanding, GMS is growing rapidly not only in Türkiye but also in foreign markets and continues its breakthroughs.

BO Commerce, established by Bayram OZMEN, a member of a family working within the construction industry for more than 30 years, aims to establish a corporate synergy with a professional team in order to contribute to the construction industry, as well as successfully completed housing projects in different provinces of Türkiye.
