About Us

BO Commerce, established by Bayram OZMEN, a member of a family working within the construction industry for more than 30 years, aims to establish a corporate synergy with a professional team in order to contribute to the construction industry, as well as successfully completed housing projects in different provinces of Türkiye.

Positioning customer satisfaction at the highest level, BO Commerce offers a one-stop opportunity to reach items required at domestic and overseas at competitive prices, as a result of solid relations with reliable solution partner supplier companies.

In arrange to respond rapidly and precisely to the requirements of the division with a policy open to development advancement, it offers diverse modern solutions to its partners when requested, in expansion to the critical brands of Turkiye, whose dealership and distribution is attempted.
